Migration of Animals

Australia

As climate change alters habitats, where will animals move to survive?

This map shows the average direction mammals, birds, and amphibians need to move to track hospitable climates.

Visualization Controls

How?

Researchers model potential habitat for species using climate change projections and the climatic needs of each species.

Using flow models from electronic circuit theory, they plot movement routes connecting current habitats with their projected locations under climate change.

Data Sources

  • Atlas of Living Australia
  • Climate projection data
